About

It's not magic. It's science — and it's overdue.

ILB Biosciences exists to replace petroleum-based plastics in everyday consumer products. Magic Materials is where we're starting — with oral care accessories that actually biodegrade.

Mission

Commercialize biopolymer materials in everyday categories — starting with oral care.

The market is ready for biodegradable products that don't require behavioral compromise. Consumers shouldn't have to choose between a product that works and a product that doesn't harm the planet.

The problem isn't consumer willingness — it's supply. We exist to build the material infrastructure, the scientific validation, and the brand signals that make genuinely sustainable products accessible at commercial scale.

The oral care accessories category is where we're starting. It's large, it's plastic-heavy, and it has no credible sustainable alternative. We think that's an opportunity.

What We Believe

Science first. Claims should follow testing, not the other way around. We don't market certifications we don't have.

No behavioral compromise. Sustainable products have to perform as well as what they replace. Consumers shouldn't notice the difference.

The co-brand model works. Ingredient brands — like Intel, Gore-Tex, Lycra — build consumer trust that benefits every brand that carries them.

Marine biodegradability is the bar. If a material doesn't biodegrade in ocean conditions, the "biodegradable" claim is incomplete at best.

The Company

ILB Biosciences

Magic Materials is the flagship commercialization program of ILB Biosciences — a company focused on bringing biopolymer materials to market in high-volume, high-turnover consumer categories.

The thesis: PHA-class biopolymers have superior environmental properties to any other commercially viable alternative to conventional plastic. The bottleneck isn't the science — it's commercialization. ILB's role is to close that gap by building the supply chain, the scientific validation infrastructure, and the brand equity that makes PHB-based products viable at scale.

Oral care accessories are category one. We expect to expand to other high-turnover single-use plastic categories as the material and brand infrastructure mature.
