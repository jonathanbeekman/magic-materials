The Problem

Oral care is one of the least-scrutinized waste streams in the home.

Billions of plastic oral care accessories are discarded every year. The so-called "eco" alternatives on the market today are mostly misleading.

🦷

~3.5 Billion

Toothbrushes discarded globally per year. Oral care accessories like floss picks and tongue scrapers add billions more.

🚫

Zero marine-safe alternatives

No commercially available floss pick or tongue scraper is genuinely marine-biodegradable at scale. Not one.

⚠️

Greenwashing is rampant

Competitors using "cornstarch + polypropylene" blends require industrial composting facilities — unavailable to most consumers. That's not biodegradable.

The Solution

Magic Materials PHA/PHB — a genuinely biodegradable material, certified by science.

We've engineered a PHB-based biopolymer compound that performs like conventional plastic and biodegrades in marine water, home compost, and soil — with no special facility required.

🌊

Marine Biodegradable

Breaks down in open ocean environments. Tested to ASTM D6691 standard via Washington State University.

🏡

Home Compostable

No industrial composting required. Biodegrades at ambient home compost temperatures — ISO 17088 certification in progress.

⚗️

Biobased

PHB (Polyhydroxybutyrate) is produced by bacterial fermentation — not petroleum. Biobased, naturally synthesized, naturally degraded.

✅

Identical Feel

Current formulation uses PHB + Nylon-6 to match the flex, rigidity, and surface feel of standard plastic accessories. We're developing a Nylon-4 (biodegradable nylon) formulation as our long-term target. Zero consumer compromise either way.
